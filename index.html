Frequently Asked Questions (F.A.Q.)

What is the purpose of vaccines?

Vaccines help protect individuals from various infectious diseases by stimulating the immune system to recognize and fight pathogens without causing the disease itself.

How many doses of each vaccine are required?

The number of doses required varies by vaccine. Some vaccines require multiple doses to build up immunity, while others may require only one dose or periodic boosters.

Are vaccines safe?

Vaccines undergo rigorous testing and continuous monitoring for safety. Most side effects are mild and temporary, such as soreness at the injection site. At Poliklinik Medan Putra (黄医生诊所), we only administer vaccines approved by the Ministry of Health (KKM) to ensure the highest standards of safety and efficacy. Rest assured that your safety is our top priority.

Can vaccines be administered together?

Many vaccines can be administered together in a single visit. Your healthcare provider will follow guidelines to ensure that multiple vaccines can be given safely.
